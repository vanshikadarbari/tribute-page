The Boy who lived

July 31 1980: Harry James Potter is born to Lily Evans Potter and James Potter.
October 31 1981: Near midnight - Voldemort attempts to kill Harry Potter after murdering his parents, but his spell rebounds. Voldemort loses most of his powers and almost his life.
September 1 1991: Harry begins first year at Hogwarts, gets sorted into Gryffindor.
September 1 1992: Harry and his friends start their second year at Hogwarts.
September 1 1993: Harry and his friends begin their third year at Hogwarts, which is now surrounded by dementors.
September 1 1994: Harry and his friends start their fourth year at Hogwarts.
September 1 1995: Harry and his friends start their fifth year at Hogwarts.
September 1 1996: Harry and his friends start their sixth year at Hogwarts.
September 1 1996: Harry and his friends start their seventh year at Hogwarts.
